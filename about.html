About Weather Alert Scheduler

Your personal weather companion for staying informed and prepared

What We Do

Weather Alert Scheduler is a comprehensive weather monitoring and alert system that helps you stay informed about weather conditions in your area. Our platform provides real-time weather data, forecasts, and customizable alerts to ensure you're always prepared for changing weather conditions.

Features

  • Real-time Weather Data: Get current weather conditions for any location worldwide
  • 5-Day Forecast: Plan ahead with detailed weather predictions
  • Custom Alerts: Set up personalized weather alerts based on your preferences
  • Multiple Alert Types: Monitor temperature, rain, snow, wind, and humidity
  • Flexible Scheduling: Choose from daily, weekly, or monthly alert frequencies
  • Weather Warnings: Receive notifications about severe weather conditions

How It Works

Our platform uses the OpenWeather API to provide accurate and up-to-date weather information. Simply enter your location, set your preferred alert conditions, and we'll notify you when those conditions are met. Whether you're planning outdoor activities, monitoring agricultural conditions, or just want to stay informed, our system has you covered.

Data Privacy

We respect your privacy and only collect the information necessary to provide weather alerts. Your location data and alert preferences are stored locally and are not shared with third parties.
